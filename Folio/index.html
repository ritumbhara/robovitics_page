RoboVITics

RoboVITics, the official robotics club of VIT, provides a base for robotics enthusiasts to progress and contribute to the field of robotics.

All year round, we work on projects and conduct events and workshops- PROJECT EXPO, COSMOS 360, MACHINE DESIGN WITH FUSION 360, HANDS-ON ROBOTICS, THE ROBOTICS CHALLENGE and obviously the ROBOWARS. We also compete in national fests conducted by various colleges in India – IIT Techfest Bombay (where we won against Brazilian Rio Botz’ Touro blasting out of the arena in a matter of 16 second), BITS Goa, and Kurukshetra’19, Anna University. (secured third place in Robowars). We act as a stepping stone for young engineers to strive and excel in robotics and automation in the future.

This year, ROBOWARS -graVITas'19 set a new benchmark for combat robotics enthusiasts on a national level. We are extremely happy and proud of the huge success of this event. It has only been possible because of the labour, great teamwork, dedication and planning put by everyone.

A two-day workshop on mobile robotics and mechatronics, in which the participants will be taught the basic drive mechanisms of autonomous robot using Arduino and various sensors and using the same to make a line follower robot, an obstacle avoider robot, a gesture controlled robot. The participants will also be given introduction on applications of image processing in robotics. The workshop will be followed by a project expo and also a competition which will provide more exposure to the participants. This workshop is open for all students from different branches and year.

A First of it’s Kind National Workshop introducing students to different features of the most futuristic, industry smart wholesome software Fusion 360. Starting with basics of part and assembly design to the most advance feature in the Design Industry ‘Generative Design’. Covering Free form modeling, CAM features, additive manufacturing along the way, with best designs getting 3-D in the end it’s an outstanding opportunity to see your designs come into life

COSMOS 360 is a National Level 24 hours CAD design hackathon based on Fusion 360 (a 3D CAD/CAM design software) brought to you by roboVITics, Autodesk aimed to provide a platform where you can unwrap your skills and discover the extents of your abilities. That's not it, roboVITics brings to you exclusive worshops by Autodesk professionals to get you ready to dream beyond the stars.
